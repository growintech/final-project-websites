ANIME OF THE MOMENT

Rating: ⭐ 9.27

After defeating the Demon King, the elven mage Frieren parts ways with her companions, to whom she is bound by a ten-year journey that, for her immortal eyes, was only a brief moment. Fifty years later, she returns to find her friends aged and facing death. The passing of the Hero Himmel leaves her with a profound regret: she never truly tried to understand the fleeting lives of humans. To remedy this, Frieren begins a new pilgrimage to the far north, seeking a land where souls rest to speak with Himmel one last time. Accompanied by her apprentice Fern and the warrior Stark, she retraces her old steps, discovering the weight of memories and the true value of human connection.
