About Us

This page was made by Anwar Miski and Lorenzo Cavallo. We noticed that in the last few years, streaming services have been rising their costs for no apparent reason. My colleague and I wanted to give a free option to those that cannot afford today's prices or do not want to subscribe to a streaming service for one singular film or TV series.
