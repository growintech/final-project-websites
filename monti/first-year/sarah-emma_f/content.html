EMMA'S TRAVELS

New York

I went to New York two years ago with a private English language school, accompanied by my best friend and her mother.
Since we only had one week to explore, we spent the entire day sightseeing.
Every day had a different itinerary, and we walked about 20 km a day.
In the evenings, we would eat at local places serving typical American food.
Despite the long flight and the endless walking, it was a magnificent trip that I will always hold dear to my heart.

1.Grand Central Terminal
2.Statue of Liberty

Kenya

My trip to Kenya was wonderful.
I travelled with my family and met new people with whom I became friends right away.
I stayed there for six days: I went on two safaris—one to see the land animals and the other to see marine life.
I spent the other four days at the beach, sunbathing, playing beach volleyball, and swimming.
I had so much fun, and I will never forget this trip.

1.Seven island resort, Watamu
2.Tsavo est national park


SARAH'S TRAVELS

Rome

In 2024, I went to Rome with my parents for two days.
For my birthday, they gave me a ticket to a Pinguini Tattici Nucleari concert.
We walked a lot and we visited the Trevi Fountain, the Imperial Fora, and the Colosseum.
Since we couldn't stay long, we didn't see everything, but it was a beautiful trip that I will remember forever.
In the photo:
1.Trajan's Column
2.Roman Forum

Romania

My trips to Romania were beautiful.
I went during the winter in 2024 and 2025.
I went with my parents and, since we went by car, it took us about 27 hours to get there and another 27 hours to get back.
We visited a lot of places and, of course, many relatives.
Each year I stayed there for two weeks and I had a lot of fun.
I went to Bocșa to see some relatives, to Brașov for a visit, and to Suceava for both.
I can't wait to go there again!
In the photo:
1.Putna Monastery
2.Brasov






OUR TRAVEL

Spain

In March 2024, we went to Spain on an Erasmus exchange for one week.
We stayed in Peal de Becerro with the students who were in Chieri for Erasmus before.
In Peal de Becerro, we saw the whole town, an olive oil mill, and Toya's Burial Chamber.
We also went to Granada, where we spent the last day and visited Alhambra.
It was a beautiful trip, and we made a lot of new friends.
