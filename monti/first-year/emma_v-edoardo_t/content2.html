Linkin Park Linkin Park redefined a genre by blending electronics, rap, and metal into a sound that gave a voice to an entire generation. At the heart of it all was Chester Bennington; his voice, capable of shifting from vulnerable whispers to soul-shattering screams, remains a beacon of authenticity that continues to provide comfort to anyone feeling lost.


For me, their best song is "Numb", I really recommend it to everyone.

Rise Against With a driving rhythmic section and lyrics charged with social commentary, Rise Against represents the beating heart of modern punk rock. Their music isn't just an adrenaline rush; it’s a constant call to action and reflection, proving that rock can still be a powerful force for change.


I recommend "Injection" it's a very energetic song and it has a driving beat. A banger!

Guns 'n Roses The undisputed symbol of wild and dangerous rock 'n' roll, Guns N' Roses dominated the world with legendary riffs and a defiant attitude. Between Slash’s iconic guitar work and Axl Rose’s magnetic charisma, they remain the ultimate blueprint for anyone seeking the raw, timeless energy of hard rock.


"Sweet child o' mine" is surely their most famous song, with a powerfull guitar riff and Axl's voice
