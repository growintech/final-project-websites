About us

Gracie Abrams Gracie Abrams is a leading figure in the "bedroom pop" and indie-pop scene. Since her debut in 2019, she has evolved from a viral sensation into a globally recognized artist, known for her intimate sound and sophisticated storytelling.

"Right now" is my favourite song from Gracie's discography.

Harry Styles Harry Styles is an English singer-songwriter, actor, and global fashion icon who has redefined the role of a modern pop star. Since embarking on his solo career, he has successfully transitioned from a teen idol into a critically acclaimed artist, blending classic rock influences with contemporary pop-soul.

Listen to"Fine line" if you want an emotional and hopeful song with a grand finale.

Chappell Roan Chappell Roan is an American pop singer known for bold, 80s-inspired queer anthems and her theatrical, drag-influenced style. After years as an independent artist, she has rapidly become a cultural phenomenon, redefining what it means to be a pop star today.

"The Subway" is a great demonstration of her vocals and her great storytelling skills.
