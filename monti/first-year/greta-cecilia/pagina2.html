Who are we?

We are Greta and Cecilia, two students who love observing the world through what people choose to wear, convinced that every outfit tells a story.


Why did we decided to create it?

The idea of Fantasy & Fashion came about almost naturally, while talking about how much fashion was changing. We noticed that more and more often, what once belonged to the world of cosplay or fantasy — bold colors, elaborate details, looks inspired by imaginary characters — was appearing on runways, on social media, and in the streets. We asked ourselves: why is nobody talking about this openly? So we decided to do it ourselves, creating a space dedicated to this cultural transformation.


What connects us to this topic?

Greta has always had an eye for style and for how the details of an outfit can completely transform a person. Cecilia is fascinated by cosplay and by how recreating a character through clothing is a true art form. Together, we represent exactly the meeting point that this site wants to explore: fashion and fantasy blending into something unique.
